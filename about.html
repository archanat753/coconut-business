About Us

Thota Archana Coconuts was started with the vision of delivering premium quality coconuts directly from farmers to customers.

Based in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh, we export fresh coconuts to Hyderabad and distribute all across India.

Our mission is to support farmers, ensure fair pricing, and provide fresh, natural products to our customers.
